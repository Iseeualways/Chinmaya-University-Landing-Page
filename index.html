About

CVV University

Deemed-to-be University

Established in 2017, Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth is Kerala’s first private university that was granted the ‘Deemed University’ status by the UGC. The mission of Chinmaya Deemed University is to offer value-based education that effectively integrates the time-tested wisdom of the east and contemporary knowledge of the west through career-focused degree programs. Chinmaya University, popularly known as the CVV University, has campuses in Kerala and Maharashtra. Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth Onakkoor campus Ernakulam, offers state-of-the-art infrastructure for higher education in an 110-acre environment-friendly campus. The new CVV Institute of Science & Technology (that offers Engineering and MBA programs) is located here.

Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth (also known as Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth or Chinmaya University) is a UGC-approved deemed-to-be university in Kerala. The Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth Onakkoor campus, located near Ernakulam, is known for its modern campus infrastructure, industry-aligned curriculum, and value-based education. CVV Ernakulam is also recognized as a NAAC-accredited university offering career-focused degree programs across engineering, commerce, management, psychology, and arts streams.

Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth is a private deemed-to-be university in Kerala and it is recognized by the UGC. It is one of the reputed private universities in Kerala and is often listed among the top colleges in Ernakulam for its academic excellence and holistic learning environment.

Chinmaya University Ernakulam offers a wide range of UG and PG courses. Courses offered in Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth include B.Tech, BCA (Hons.), BBA (Hons.), B.Com (Hons.), B.Sc (Hons.), BA, Integrated B.Ed, MBA, MCA, M.Sc, and MA. The university is especially known for Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth Psychology, engineering programs under CVV Institute of Science and Technology, and MBA specializations aligned with industry needs.

Chinmaya University Ernakulam is emerging as one of the best BCA colleges in Kerala and among the preferred BCA colleges in Ernakulam. The university offers BCA (Hons.) programs including AI-focused specializations, making it relevant for students searching for BCA AI colleges in Kerala. The curriculum is designed to be career-focused and aligned with current industry demands.

The Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth campus at Onakkoor offers modern campus infrastructure, digital classrooms, hostel facilities, and a holistic learning environment. The hostel facilities provide safe and comfortable accommodation for students. Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth scholarships are available for meritorious UG and PG students in Kerala. The university also emphasizes Chinmaya Vishwa Vidyapeeth placement support through industry partnerships, internships, and career-focused degree programs, making it a strong choice among top colleges in Ernakulam and private universities in Kerala.

All classes and learning materials are available on a dedicated online platform on Amrita Online with 24/7 access. The programs follow a flexible learning approach, combining video content, live sessions, and digital resources.
